SITARE ZAMEEN PAR (2025)

About The Movie

Sitaare Zameen Par (lit. 'Stars on Earth') is a 2025 Indian Hindi-language sports comedy-drama film directed by R. S. Prasanna, and produced by Aamir Khan and Aparna Purohit. It is a spiritual successor to Khan's 2007 film Taare Zameen Par, and stars him and Genelia Deshmukh.[5][6][7] It is an official remake of the 2018 Spanish film Champions,[8] and follows a suspended basketball coach who must serve community service by helping a team of players with disabilities prepare for a tournament.
The film was announced in October 2023.[10] Principal photography took place in India over four months before completing in June 2024.
Sitaare Zameen Par was released on 20 June 2025.[13] The film grossed ₹266 crore worldwide and emerged as the sixth highest-grossing Hindi film of 2025 as well as the eleventh highest-grossing Indian film of 2025.
